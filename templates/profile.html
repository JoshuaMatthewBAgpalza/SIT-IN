Laboratory Rules and Regulations
To maintain proper conduct in our laboratories:
  • Maintain silence, proper decorum, and discipline inside the laboratory. Mobile phones and other personal devices must be silenced.
  • Games are not allowed inside the lab, including computer games that may disrupt the lab environment.
  • Internet use is restricted to educational purposes with instructor permission. No downloading or installing software.
  • Accessing non-academic websites (especially inappropriate content) is strictly prohibited.
  • Do not delete computer files or alter computer settings.
  • Food, drinks, gum, and smoking are prohibited inside the laboratory.
Disciplinary Actions:
  • First Offense: Referral to Guidance Center for possible class suspension.
  • Subsequent Offenses: More severe sanctions may be imposed.
Sit-in Rules
Please observe the following sit-in rules:
  • Timing: Allowed from Midterm period onwards, with exceptions during Prelims.
  • Entry Protocol: Only permitted 15 minutes after class begins, with instructor permission (except during vacant hours).
  • Proper Contact: Approach laboratory staff (not instructors) before entering.
  • ID Submission: Present your ID to laboratory staff and state your intended activities.
  • Conduct: Maintain silence while working to avoid disrupting classes.
  • Exit Procedure: Save files, close applications, log off, arrange seating, and collect your ID before leaving.
